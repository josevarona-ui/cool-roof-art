The shift

Flight paths are millions of eyeballs pointed at your roof.

On approach, passengers stare out the window with nothing to look at but rooftops. That attention has always been wasted on gray tar and gravel. We turn the roof into the reason they look — a landmark from the air, on satellite, and on every map.

50M+passengers a year descend over Miami's rooftops into MIA.
Flight path → attention → your roof

The second vantage

Not just from the sky — from every tower that looks down on it.

Flight paths are one audience. Downtowns are the other. In dense cores like Downtown Miami and Brickell, thousands of office, hotel, and condo windows sit above street level — and every one of them looks down onto the shorter rooftops nearby.

A blank roof wastes that permanent, captive view. A mural owns it — turning a neighbor's window into a gallery seat.

Wherever your building sits, someone is looking down on it — from the planes on approach to MIA, or from the towers next door. Cool Roof Art makes sure what they see is worth the look.

High-rise sightlines → your roof

Found on the map

The first thing they see is the satellite view.

Before anyone visits, they look you up — and Google Maps opens on the aerial image of your address. For most buildings that's a gray blur no one remembers. With a Gerada mural on the roof, your address looks like something the moment it loads.

Every search for your location, every set of directions, every satellite pass that refreshes the imagery puts your art back in front of people — long after the paint is dry, at no extra cost.

A landmark works around the clock. The plane passes in seconds; the map is forever.

What we do

Two outcomes from one surface.

Most roofs are dead space that bakes in the sun. We make yours do two jobs at once.

01 — PERFORMANCE

A certified cool roof

Reflective, code-recognized coatings that lower surface temperature, ease the load on HVAC, and extend the life of the membrane underneath.

02 — IDENTITY

A mural by a world-renowned artist

Your roof becomes an original work by Jorge Rodríguez-Gerada — a Cuban-American artist whose large-scale murals and land art are exhibited worldwide and, at their scale, visible from space.

The artist

Signed by Jorge Rodríguez-Gerada.

Our co-founder is a Cuban-American contemporary artist recognized around the world for monumental murals and land art — works so vast that some are photographed by satellites and visible from space. His portraits have stood beside the White House on the National Mall, risen across city walls from Barcelona to Belfast, and honored frontline workers in New York.

Cool Roof Art puts that same hand on your roof. Not a flat, single-color coating and not a print — but an original, permanent artwork by an internationally exhibited artist, hand-painted in UV-stable pigment and built to be seen from the sky.
